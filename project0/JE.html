What they do

Founded in 2001, Just Eat is a pioneer of the online takeaway food delivery market and continues to lead innovation across the industry. As a market leader their brands connect millions of customers with more than 100,000 local Restaurant Partners serving over 100 different cuisines, providing an unrivalled choice of food that people love.

Their purpose sits at the heart of everything they do and that’s to make food discovery exciting for everyone, whether it’s by giving our customers more choice than anywhere else, or by supporting our Restaurant Partners to get more out of their business.

They are Just Eat

Just Eat operates a leading global hybrid marketplace for online food delivery, providing customers with an easy and secure way to order and pay for food from Just Eats Restaurant Partners.

For their customers, it is about offering them the widest choice – whatever, whenever and wherever they want to eat.

For their Restaurant Partners, they help them to reach more customers, support their businesses and improve standards in the industry.

For their people, it is being part of a global team, helping to connect 26 million active customers with our 100,000 Restaurant Partners.
